Feed the Desert

Feed the Desert is an effort to identify food deserts! It uses a geodatabase to locate and call attention to communities at greater risk of weight-related health conditions and food-scarcity. We hope that this information helps stores that offer produce to decide where to expand; communities to find their local stores, community gardens, and farmer's markets; and jurisdictions to target where to focus their long-term infrastructural and transit planning.
